Board of Hostel Management (BHM)

The Board for Hostel Management provides a welcoming home where students create memories, share ideas, and grow alongside friends. It coordinates the working of all the hostels and takes decisions on all policies of common interest. The Associate Dean Hostel Management, in his capacity as the President of the Board for Hostel Management, is the head of the hostel organisation..
